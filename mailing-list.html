Mailing lists for ACL SIGUR the special interest group of Uralistics

The SIG members can opt in to get the messages and discussions from the SIG mailing list (less than 1 a month). The list is handled through google groups and you will get an invite after filling in the form, which you will have accept to receive the messages. Alternately you can go to https://groups.google.com to manage your google groups.
